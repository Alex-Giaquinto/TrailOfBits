About Me

The main reason I coach is for my coach Pat Sempier. A man that completely flipped my life, gave me my identity, and allowed me to get the stage that I am at today. I dedicate everything I do on the football field to him and wanted you as the client to know that.

The foundation of my teaching is based on the simple statement "Head down, drive up." This means to keep your eyes down, and drive your leg up and through the middle of the uprights. This statement alone completely changed the game for me in high school and will change your life if you chose to work with us.
